A show and tell for hackers, makers and tinkerers of all shapes and kinds

NewLine is back for its 2026 edition! Join us for three days of creativity, technology and community at Hackerspace Gent.

Whether you're building robots, writing code, soldering circuits, designing 3D models or tinkering with anything in between — this is your space to share your passion and learn from others.

How to get there

Wiedauwkaai 51, 9000 Gent, Belgium

Brussels Airport (BRU) — 55 km, main international hub. Direct trains to Gent-Sint-Pieters (~1 hour).

Brussels South Charleroi Airport (CRL) — 100 km, budget airlines. Direct flibco shuttle bus to Gent.

Gent is well-connected by train. The main station is Gent-Sint-Pieters.

From Gent-Sint-Pieters to Hackerspace Gent:

Tram T4 — Get off at Muidebrug

Bus 55 — Get off at Wiedauwkaai
